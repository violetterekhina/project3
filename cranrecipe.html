Cranberry Cookies

Ingredients

3 eggs
2 cups sugar
3/4 cup butter, softened
1 tsp vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries

GLAZE:
2 cups powdered sugar
2 tbsp fresh lemon juice,
(+ more as desired)


Est. preparation time: 40min

1.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. With a mixer, beat the eggs with the sugar for about 5 minutes. This mixture should almost double in size, so do not skip this step even though it is tedious! Add the butter and vanilla and mix two more minutes. Stir in the flour until just combined. Add the cranberries and stir
to mix throughout.

2.

Use a 2 tablespoon scoop or a spoon to portion out the cookie dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Place only six cookies on the baking sheet. Bake for 18 minutes, until the cookies have puffed up and turned golden brown on the edges. Remove from the oven and cool on the baking sheet for at least 5 min before transfering to a wire rack to finish cooling.

3.

To glaze the cookies, stir together the powdered sugar and lemon juice. It should drizzle off the spoon in a thick white stream. Lightly drizzle the cookies and allow them to fully set before storing.

Enjoy!
