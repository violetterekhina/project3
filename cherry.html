Cherry

As most other berries, cherries promote the health of the heart and cardiovascular system. They may reduce blood pressure and improve sleep since they contain melatonin. Cherries also help manage our level of cholesterol and enhance recovery after exercising.

In 1 cup of cherries...

Copper

9% of DV*

Supports the immune system

Acts as an antioxidant

Creates red blood cells

Keeps nerve cells healthy

Turns sugar into energy

Vitamin C

12% of DV*

Boosts immunity

Protects cells and keeps them healthy

Maintains healthy skin, blood vessels, bones and cartilage

Accelerates wound healing

Reduce risk of chronic disease

Helps reduce stress

Potassium

7% of DV*

Helps move nutrients and waste
around the body's cells

responsible for muscle contraction

Helps regulate heartbeat and blood pressure.

Maintains normal levels of fluid inside cells.

Potassium deficiency may look like weakness, fatigue, excessive thirst and muscle aches.
*DV = recommended daily intake value
