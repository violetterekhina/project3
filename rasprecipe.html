Raspberry Honey Cake

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
1 cup canola oil
1 cup honey
3 large eggs
1/2 cup orange juice
1 tsp vanilla extract
3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 cup fresh or frozen raspberries

GLAZE:
1 cup sugar
2 tbsp honey
2 tbsp milk


Est. preparation time: 1 hr 5 min

1.

Preheat the oven to 350°. In a large bowl, beat sugar, oil, honey, eggs, orange juice and vanilla until well blended. In another bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt, then gradually beat into sugar mixture. Spray your pan with oil and put the batter into it. Bake for 15 minutes.

2.

After 15 minutes, take out your cake and sprinkle it with raspberries. Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, (about 30-35 more minutes). Cool completely before adding the glaze.

3.

Combine all the glaze ingredients till smooth and drizzle it over cake. Serve with vanilla ice cream and/or additional berries that you may have left over.

Enjoy!
