Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1 cups granulated sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 eggs
1/2 cup butter melted
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups blueberries

STREUSEL TOPPING:
1/4 cup flour
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp granulated sugar
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/8 tsp salt
2 tbsp butter cold

Est. preparation time: 35min

1.

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and teaspoon salt. Set aside. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs until smooth.

2.

Add the melted butter, milk, and vanilla, and whisk until smooth. Add the egg mixture to the flour mixture and stir until combined and no lumps are left. Fold in blueberries. Mix the ingredients for the streusel topping except for the butter. Slowly cut the butter into the mixture until it is crumbly and coarse.

3.

Fill your muffin cups with the batter and top each muffin with the topping (about 1 tbsp of the mixture for each). Bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until the muffin centers bounce back when lightly pressed, Cool muffins on a rack for several minutes before removing from pan.

Enjoy!
