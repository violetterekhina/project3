Cherry Pie

Ingredients

1.5 cups frozen or fresh cherries
1 egg
1 cup + 3 tbsp sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp salt
1 ¼ cup flour


Est. preparation time: 50min

1.

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Whisk eggs with salt, vanilla extract and 1 cup of sugar. Add 1 cup of flour into the mix. Switch to using your hands once the dough becomes too thick to keep whisking. Set the dough to chill in the fridge for about 30 mins. After that, separate it into three equally sized pieces.

2.

Roll out one of the pieces into a circle or square, depending on the shape of your baking pan. Grease the pan and place the dough base in it. Next, use the second 1/3 of dough to make the walls of the pie, evenly spreading the dough around the sides of the pan. Place the cherries into the pie, add 3 tbps of sugar evenly on top of the cherries.

3.

Add the ¼ cup of flour into the last bit of dough. We want this one to be a bit thicker. In the end it should have the consistency of cookie crumble. Adjust flour amount based on your discretion. Spread the crumble evenly on top of the pie. Bake for 30-40 min, serve hot or cold.

Enjoy!
